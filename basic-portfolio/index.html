About Me

Hi! My name is Artawood. Nice to meet you. It started 5 years ago with helping a friend on her class project for web design. Since then, I've been freelancing off and on, working collaboratively with designers on website projects for various clients. My journey started with simple html pages. And then it evolved to CSS. Eventually I took a trip into UX/UI Design, all the while doing my day job as a Research Administrator at UC Berkeley.

I've reached a juncture in my life to finally take a different route to being a full time UX/UI Designer and Front End Developer.
